Tips for a Healthy Lifestyle

We have already conveyed so much about technology, but due you know that with increasing dependency on Technology a lot of people always complains about health issues, so here few tips are mentioned for healthy lifestyle with technology.

1. Don't forget to do Yoga and Exercises everyday, atleast 30 minutes of walk is needed.

2. Drink enough water and don't skip meal.

3. Try not to sit too long infront of screen, maintain 1-hand distance while looking at screen.

4. Take break and walk in between work, avoid sitting for longer hours.

5. Take proper sleep and maintain relations with family and friends, they will listen to your stress and will help you out with the proper solutions.
